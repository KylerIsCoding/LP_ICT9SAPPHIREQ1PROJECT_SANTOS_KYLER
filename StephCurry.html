Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

Characters

Miles Morales

Miles Morales is a teenager with spider-like abilities, who takes up the mantle of Spider-Man.

Peter B. Parker

Peter B. Parker is an older, more experienced Spider-Man who becomes a mentor to Miles.

Peter Porker (Spider-Ham)

Peter Porker, also known as Spider-Ham, is a humorous, pig-like version of Spider-Man from an alternate dimension.
